Many players have been asking how to get some of the custom emojis also known as Unicode or glyphs. Here is an in-depth guide on how to obtain and use these awesome characters. Use this website as your atlas to find the Unicode. Below is a chart of the current locations of the in-game Unicode. Once you have found the Unicode you would like to use, copy and paste it into your Book and Quill.

Open the Unicode Explorer from the button above. You will see plenty of Unicode values.

Scroll to find values ranging from E000 to EFFF. Click the value you want to copy.

Click copy on the value page. It should say "Copied to clipboard".

Open Minecraft, join CraftersMC, and paste the Unicode value into your Book and Quill.

Irrl's Glyph

Click the button above to access glyphs for copying. After loading, click on the glyph you want, and it will be copied to your clipboard. Paste it in your Book and Quill in CraftersMC.

Not all Unicode values will work. E400-407 are store ranks, whereas E420-425 are staff ranks. If nothing appears, try a different value.

Pasting "E400" etc will NOT WORK. You MUST copy a Unicode value using the websites given above.
